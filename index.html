Top 5 Fortnite sweat skins

Last but not least, at number 5 we have Mogul Master.

The Mogul Master is an epic skin that was released in Chapter 1 Season 2, and is part of the winter ski set

The Mogul Master Fortnite skins present a collection of formidable characters, each representing a different snowy mountain region with unique styles and personalities. From the frosty peaks of the Alps to the rugged slopes of the Rockies, these skins embody the spirit of winter sports and mountain mastery. There are 8 Different variations of this skin that come at a hefty price tag of 1500 Vbucks. These variantions include, China, Korea, Usa, Great Britain, Germany, France, and Canada. The canadian version is Nick Eh 30's Signature skin in which he uses in most videos.

At number 4 we have Echo.

Echo is a rare Fortnite skin that was introduced in Chapter 2 Season 2, it is part of the twin echo set.

Echo has four syles, originally it only had two being silver and black and a reverse option. Later on they added two purple styles that look quite similar except for the slighty different shade and hands. These both look pretty cool and are commonly used by many pros.

Coming in at number 3 we have Aura.

Aura is a uncommon Fortnite skin that was introduced in Chapter 1 season 8,it is part of the gilded explores bundle.

Aura has three styles, these consist of the default, one with a wolly jumper, and one with an alternate colourway. This skin is very common to see in game as it only costs 800 vbucks and many people like the design and look the skin has. It has been very popular for many years and probably many more to come, the only reason this isn't number 1 is because there are many bots who use this skin.

Number 2 we have Dynamo/the mongraal skin.

Dynamo is a rare Fortnite skin that was released in Chapter 1 season 5, and is part of the Lucha set.

Dynamo is commonly know as the "Mongraal skin" because of how he made different skin so popular. It comes at a fair price of 1200 vbucks and if you want a super clean combo consider picking up the candy axe, although this combo will cost you 2700 altogether. Many sweats still use this skin to this day and if you ever see one consider running away. If you want to pick this skin up it returns about every 60 days or so, but the candy axe returns every christmas.

At number 1 we have superhero skins/boundless set.

These superhero skins are all incredibly scary and were released in chapter 2 season 4 during the Marvel season.

Superhero skins are the peak of try-hard skins, it seems like every single pro uses these because of the ability to customize the skin so that they can match/blend in with their surroundings. Although there is a nearly endless colour combination customization, most players just seem to either go for plain white or black because of the advandtage these give. Every skin in the boundless set costs 1800 vbucks and comes with a build in emote and an emoticon, which is quite good value for what you're getting. If you would want to pick one of these skins up you may have to wait a little since sometimes it is quite inconsistent on when they return.
